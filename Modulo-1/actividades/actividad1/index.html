Omelett Recipe

Classic Omelett

Prep

5 mins

Cook

5 mins

Total

10 mins

Ingredients

4 large eggs room temperature

1/4 cup whole milk

1/4 teaspoon kosher salt

2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Instructions

Beat the eggs and milk until light and frothy.

Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the butter.

Pour in the eggs. Let them set for a few seconds.

Push the cooked eggs from the edge of the skillet toward the center using a spatula.

Fold the omelette in half when the eggs are mostly set but still soft.

Slide the omelette onto a plate.
